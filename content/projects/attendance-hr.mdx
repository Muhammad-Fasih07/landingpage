## Overview

An internal Attendance & HR portal used for clock-in/out, shifts, employee records, and operational reporting. Live at [ams.globaldigitsolutions.com](https://ams.globaldigitsolutions.com/).

## Architecture

- **Frontend:** React dashboards for Admin, HR, and Employee roles
- **Backend:** Node.js / Express REST APIs
- **Data:** SQL and MongoDB depending on domain needs
- **Access:** Role-based permissions across sensitive HR surfaces

## Responsibilities

- Implemented real-time attendance tracking flows
- Built reporting and records management UI
- Deployed and maintained cloud-hosted environments

## Outcome

Replaced manual tracking with a centralized portal for daily attendance and HR operations.
